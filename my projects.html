Sentiment Analysis 😊

▼

A real-time web app that analyzes your mood and displays an animated emoji based on your emotions! 💬🎭 Simply type in a message, and the app reflects your sentiment (happy, sad, angry, or neutral) using a floating emoji over a dynamic 3D background.

  • Emotion-aware UI/UX
  • 3D animated emoji feedback
  • Real-time sentiment detection
  • Applications in chatbots, mental health tools, and feedback systems

    Astra ✋

    ▼

    🖐️ ASTRA – Hands-Free Operating System ASTRA is an innovative gesture-based system that allows users to control their computer without touching any hardware. Using hand gestures and eye blinks, users can navigate, click, scroll, adjust volume/brightness, and even open apps — completely hands-free. 💡 Powered by Computer Vision and OpenCV, this project enhances accessibility and introduces a futuristic way to interact with digital systems. 🌟 ASTRA was recognized for its real-time responsiveness and social impact potential, making it a standout at various tech expos.
